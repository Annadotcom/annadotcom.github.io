About me

I am Phuoc B. T. Huynh. I received my Ph.D. in August 2025 and currently work as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Beyond-G Global Innovation Center, Hanyang University. My research focuses on RF integrated circuits (RFIC), phase-locked loops (PLLs), wideband LNAs, and noise-cancelling techniques. I aim to develop low-power, high-performance RF circuits enabling 6G-and-beyond wireless systems.
