Research Interests

  • RF Integrated Circuits (RFIC): low-power, high-performance building blocks for wireless front-ends.
  • Wideband LNAs: noise-cancelling techniques, linearization (dual-resistive feedback), inductorless & transformer-based design.
  • Phase-Locked Loops (PLLs): fast-locking architectures, low-power PFD/CP, phase-error compensation (DAPD).
  • 6G-and-Beyond Systems: RFIC techniques enabling robust, energy-efficient radios.
  • Measurement & Modeling: noise figure, linearity, frequency stability, process-voltage-temperature robustness.
