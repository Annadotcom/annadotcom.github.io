Research Interests

Low-Noise Amplifiers (LNA)

  • Wideband CMOS LNA design with noise-cancelling and robust linearization.
  • Architectures: dual-resistive feedback, current reuse, and inductive loading for simultaneous noise & input matching.
  • Target: low-power, high-linearity front-ends for 6G-class radios (e.g., 0.05–4 GHz wideband coverage as demonstrated in prior work).

Phase-Locked Loops (PLL) & RFIC for 6G

  • Energy-efficient RF building blocks enabling dense integration and multi-band operation.
  • Design strategies emphasizing low power, high performance, and system-level robustness.
